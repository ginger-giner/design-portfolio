FEEL SOMETHING ALREADY

Affect is a series that depicts the experience of seeing life through a phone. Are we obscuring our connections with other humans? Does our drive to capture memories ultimately keep us an arm's length away from the moments that we collect? The moments captured are now gone.

They're only stories we can tell ourselves. Would these memories be more tangible had we not photographed them? Perhaps the mind inherently de-prioritizes the things we document, our brains comfortable with erasing those things we have sitting safely as 1's and 0's, hard data.
Sam the Creative Dude
